About

Overview

This workshop covered recent advances at the exciting intersection of artificial intelligence and quantum computing, focusing on novel algorithms and their applications.

Researchers and practitioners discussed the latest developments and future directions in this rapidly evolving field, spanning hardware integration, AI-assisted algorithm design, scalability of quantum-classical hybrid systems, and error mitigation techniques.

The workshop explored how AI techniques can be integrated with quantum algorithms — addressing hybrid quantum computing, scalable AI-quantum workflows, and current trends in quantum software and systems.
